Frequently asked questions

Quick answers while we finish checkout on the site.

Where do you ship?

United States addresses only (including Alaska and Hawaii where carriers and service levels allow). We do not ship internationally at this time.

How do returns work?

Eligible unworn pieces may be returned within 7 days of delivery, subject to inspection. Read the full policy on Shipping and Returns.

Do you charge sales tax?

Where required, applicable state and local sales tax will be collected on your order. The exact amount depends on your shipping address and will be shown before you confirm purchase once checkout is live.

How can I reach you?

Email contact@ecloire.com for sizing help, order questions, or press.
